# trac -- reStructuredText breach of privacy and denial of service vulnerability
The Trac 0.9.6 Release Notes reports:

> Fixed reStructuredText breach of privacy and denial of service
> vulnerability found by Felix Wiemann.
>
> The discovered vulnerability requires docutils to be installed and
> enabled. Systems that do not have docutils installed or enabled are
> not vulnerable. As of this version version 0.3.9 or greater of
> docutils is required for using reStructuredText markup in Trac.


## Affected packages
 - **trac**
    - **Fixed:** 0.9.6
 - **ja-trac**
    - **Fixed:** 0.9.6
