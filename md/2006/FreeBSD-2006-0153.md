# drupal-pubcookie -- authentication may be bypassed
The Drupal Project reports:

> It is possible for a malicious user to spoof a user\'s identity by
> bypassing the login redirection mechanism in the pubcookie module. The
> malicious user may gain the privileges of the user they are spoofing,
> including the administrative user.


## Affected packages
 - **drupal-pubcookie**
    - **Fixed:** 4.6.0_20060210
