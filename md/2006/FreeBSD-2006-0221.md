# dbus -- match_rule_equal() Weakness
Secunia reports:

> D-Bus have a weakness, which can be exploited by malicious, local
> users to cause a DoS (Denial of Service).
>
> An error within the \"match_rule_equal()\" function can be exploited
> to disable the ability of other processes to receive messages by
> removing their matches from D-Bus.


## Affected packages
 - **dbus**
    - **Fixed:** 1.0.2
