# frr -- BGP Mishandled attribute length on Error
FRR reports:

> BGP Mishandled attribute length on Error
>
> A vulnerability exists in the BGP daemon of FRR where a malformed BGP
> UPDATE packet can leak information from the BGP daemon and cause a
> denial of service by crashing the daemon.


## Affected packages
 - **frr**
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.2
