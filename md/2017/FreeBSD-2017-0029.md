# icoutils -- check_offset overflow on 64-bit systems
Choongwoo Han reports:

> An exploitable crash exists in the wrestool utility on 64-bit systems
> where the result of subtracting two pointers exceeds the size of int.


## Affected packages
 - **icoutils**
    - **Fixed:** 0.31.1
