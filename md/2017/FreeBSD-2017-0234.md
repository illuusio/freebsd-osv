# xorg-server -- multiple vulnerabilities
Alan Coopersmith reports:

> X.Org thanks Michal Srb of SuSE for finding these issues and bringing
> them to our attention, Julien Cristau of Debian for getting the fixes
> integrated, and Adam Jackson of Red Hat for publishing the release.


## Affected packages
 - **xephyr**
    - **Fixed:** 1.18.4_4,1
 - **xorg-dmx**
    - **Fixed:** 1.18.4_4,1
 - **xorg-nestserver**
    - **Fixed:** 1.19.1_1,2
 - **xorg-server**
    - **Fixed:** 1.18.4_4,1
 - **xorg-vfbserver**
    - **Fixed:** 1.19.1_1,1
 - **xwayland**
    - **Fixed:** 1.19.1_1
