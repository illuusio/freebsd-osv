# ansible -- information disclosure flaw
ansible developers report:

> Ansible versions 2.2.3 and earlier are vulnerable to an information
> disclosure flaw due to the interaction of call back plugins and the
> no_log directive where the information may not be sanitized properly.


## Affected packages
 - **ansible**
    - **Fixed:** 2.2.3
