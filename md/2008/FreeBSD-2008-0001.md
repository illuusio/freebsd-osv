# linux-flashplugin -- multiple vulnerabilities
Adobe Security bulletin:

> Critical vulnerabilities have been identified in Adobe Flash Player
> that could allow an attacker who successfully exploits these potential
> vulnerabilities to take control of the affected system. A malicious
> SWF must be loaded in Flash Player by the user for an attacker to
> exploit these potential vulnerabilities. Users are recommended to
> update to the most current version of Flash Player available for their
> platform.


## Affected packages
 - **linux-flashplugin**
    - **Introduced:** 9.0
    - **Fixed:** 9.0r115
    - **Introduced:** 7.0
    - **Fixed:** 7.0r73
