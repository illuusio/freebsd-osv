# Gallery 1.4.3 and ealier user authentication bypass
A flaw exists in Gallery versions previous to 1.4.3-pl1 and post 1.2
which may give an attacker the potential to log in under the \"admin\"
account. Data outside of the gallery is unaffected and the attacker
cannot modify any data other than the photos or photo albums.


## Affected packages
 - **gallery**
    - **Fixed:** 1.4.3.2
