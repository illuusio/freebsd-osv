# leafnode denial-of-service triggered by article request
The leafnode NNTP server may go into an unterminated loop with 100% CPU
use when an article is requested by Message-ID that has been crossposted
to several news groups when one of the group names is the prefix of
another group name that the article was cross-posted to. Found by Jan
Knutar.


## Affected packages
 - **leafnode**
    - **Introduced:** 1.9.20
    - **Fixed:** 1.9.30
