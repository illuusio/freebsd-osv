# zinf -- potential buffer overflow playlist support
The audio player Zinf is vulnerable to a buffer-overflow bug in the
management of the playlist files.


## Affected packages
 - **zinf**
    - **Fixed:** 2.2.5
 - **freeamp**
    - **Introduced:** 0,1
