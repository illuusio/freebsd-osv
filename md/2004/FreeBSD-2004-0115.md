# isc-dhcp3-server buffer overflow in logging mechanism
A buffer overflow exists in the logging functionality of the DHCP daemon
which could lead to Denial of Service attacks and has the potential to
allow attackers to execute arbitrary code.


## Affected packages
 - **isc-dhcp3-relay**
    - **Introduced:** 3.0.1.r12
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.1.r14
 - **isc-dhcp3-server**
    - **Introduced:** 3.0.1.r12
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.1.r14
