# zebra/quagga denial of service vulnerability
A remote attacker could cause zebra/quagga to crash by sending a
malformed telnet command to their management port.


## Affected packages
 - **zebra**
    - **Fixed:** 0.93b_7
 - **quagga**
    - **Fixed:** 0.96.4
