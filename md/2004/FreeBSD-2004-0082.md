# leafnode fetchnews denial-of-service triggered by missing header
Fetchnews could hang when a news article to be downloaded lacked one of
the mandatory headers. Found by Joshua Crawford.


## Affected packages
 - **leafnode**
    - **Introduced:** 1.9.3
    - **Fixed:** 1.9.41
