# MySQL -- Multiple vulnerabilities
Oracle reports:

> The 2022 April Critical Patch Update contains 43 new security patches
> for Oracle MySQL. 11 of these vulnerabilities may be remotely
> exploitable without authentication, i.e., may be exploited over a
> network without requiring user credentials.


## Affected packages
 - **mysql57-server**
    - **Fixed:** 5.7.38
 - **mysql80-client**
    - **Fixed:** 8.0.29
 - **mysql80-server**
    - **Fixed:** 8.0.29
