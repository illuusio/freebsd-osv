# Grafana -- Plugin signature bypass
Grafana Labs reports:

> On July 4th as a result of an internal security audit we have
> discovered a bypass in the plugin signature verification by exploiting
> a versioning flaw.
>
> We believe that this vulnerability is rated at CVSS 6.1
> (CVSS:3.1/AV:L/AC:L/PR:H/UI:R/S:U/C:H/I:H/A:L).


## Affected packages
 - **grafana**
    - **Introduced:** 7.0.0
    - **Fixed:** 8.5.14
    - **Introduced:** 9.0.0
    - **Fixed:** 9.1.8
 - **grafana7**
    - **Introduced:** 7.0.0
 - **grafana8**
    - **Introduced:** 8.0.0
    - **Fixed:** 8.5.14
 - **grafana9**
    - **Introduced:** 9.0.0
    - **Fixed:** 9.1.8
