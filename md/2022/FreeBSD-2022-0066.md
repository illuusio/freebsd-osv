# gitea -- Open Redirect on login
Andrew Thornton reports:

> When a location containing backslashes is presented, the existing
> protections against open redirect are bypassed, because browsers will
> convert adjacent forward and backslashes within the location to double
> forward slashes.


## Affected packages
 - **gitea**
    - **Fixed:** 1.16.5
