# zeek -- potential DoS vulnerabilities
Tim Wojtulewicz of Corelight reports:

> A specially-crafted series of HTTP 0.9 packets can cause Zeek to spend
> large amounts of time processing the packets.
>
> A specially-crafted FTP packet can cause Zeek to spend large amounts
> of time processing the command.
>
> A specially-crafted IPv6 packet can cause Zeek to overflow memory and
> potentially crash.


## Affected packages
 - **zeek**
    - **Fixed:** 5.0.4
