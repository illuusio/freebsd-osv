# e2fsprogs -- out-of-bounds read/write vulnerability
Nils Bars reports:

> During the processing of \[a specially fuzzed disk image\], an
> out-of-bounds write is triggered and causes a segmentation fault
> (SIGSEGV).


## Affected packages
 - **e2fsprogs**
    - **Fixed:** 1.46.5_1
 - **e2fsprogs-nobootfsck**
    - **Fixed:** 1.46.5_1
 - **e2fsprogs-roothardlinks**
    - **Fixed:** 1.46.5_1
