# py-numpy -- Missing return-value validation of the function PyArray_DescrNew
Numpy reports:

> At most call-sites for PyArray_DescrNew, there are no validations of
> its return, but an invalid address may be returned.


## Affected packages
 - **py38-numpy**
    - **Fixed:** 1.22.4
 - **py39-numpy**
    - **Fixed:** 1.22.4
 - **py310-numpy**
    - **Fixed:** 1.22.4
