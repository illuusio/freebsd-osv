# mutt -- mutt_decode_uuencoded() can read past the of the input line
Tavis Ormandy reports:

> mutt_decode_uuencoded(), the line length is read from the untrusted
> uuencoded part without validation. This could result in including
> private memory in message parts, for example fragments of other
> messages, passphrases or keys in replys


## Affected packages
 - **mutt**
    - **Fixed:** 2.2.3
