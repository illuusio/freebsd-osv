# dendrite -- Incorrect parsing of the event default power level in event auth
Dendrite team reports:

> The power level parsing within gomatrixserverlib was failing to parse
> the \"events_default\" key of the m.room.power_levels event,
> defaulting the event default power level to zero in all cases.
>
> In rooms where the \"events_default\" power level had been changed,
> this could result in events either being incorrectly authorised or
> rejected by Dendrite servers.


## Affected packages
 - **dendrite**
    - **Fixed:** 0.9.3
