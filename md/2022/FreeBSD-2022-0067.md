# dnsmasq -- heap use-after-free in dhcp6_no_relay
Petr Menšík reports:

> Possible vulnerability \[\...\] found in latest dnsmasq. It \[was\]
> found with help of oss-fuzz Google project by me and short after that
> independently also by Richard Johnson of Trellix Threat Labs.
>
> It is affected only by DHCPv6 requests, which could be crafted to
> modify already freed memory. \[\...\] We think it might be triggered
> remotely, but we do not think it could be used to execute remote code.


## Affected packages
 - **dnsmasq**
    - **Fixed:** 2.86_4,1
 - **dnsmasq-devel**
    - **Fixed:** 2.86_4,1
