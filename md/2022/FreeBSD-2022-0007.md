# clamav -- invalid pointer read that may cause a crash
Laurent Delosieres reports:

> Fix for invalid pointer read that may cause a crash. This issue
> affects 0.104.1, 0.103.4 and prior when ClamAV is compiled with
> libjson-c and the `CL_SCAN_GENERAL_COLLECT_METADATA` scan option (the
> `clamscan --gen-json` option) is enabled.


## Affected packages
 - **clamav**
    - **Fixed:** 0.104.2,1
 - **clamav-lts**
    - **Fixed:** 0.103.5,1
