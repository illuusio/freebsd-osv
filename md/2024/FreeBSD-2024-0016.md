# zeek -- potential DoS vulnerability
Tim Wojtulewicz of Corelight reports:

> A specially-crafted series of packets containing nested MIME entities
> can cause Zeek to spend large amounts of time parsing the entities.


## Affected packages
 - **zeek**
    - **Fixed:** 6.0.3
