# zeek -- potential DoS vulnerability
Tim Wojtulewicz of Corelight reports:

> Large QUIC packets can cause Zeek to overflow memory and potentially
> crash. Due to the possibility of receiving these packets from remote
> hosts, this is a DoS risk.


## Affected packages
 - **zeek**
    - **Fixed:** 7.0.5
