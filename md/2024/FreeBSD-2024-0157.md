# frr - Multiple vulnerabilities
cve@mitre.org reports:

> In FRRouting (FRR) through 9.1, there are multiples vulnerabilities.
>
> - CVE-2024-31950: buffer overflow and daemon crash in ospf_te_parse_ri
>   for OSPF LSA packets
> - CVE-2024-31951: buffer overflow and daemon crash in
>   ospf_te_parse_ext_link for OSPF LSA packets


## Affected packages
 - **frr9**
    - **Fixed:** 9.1.1
 - **frr8**
    - **Fixed:** 8.5.5
