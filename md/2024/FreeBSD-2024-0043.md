# FreeBSD -- jail(2) information leak
# Problem Description:

The jail(2) system call has not limited a visiblity of allocated TTYs
(the kern.ttys sysctl). This gives rise to an information leak about
processes outside the current jail.

# Impact:

Attacker can get information about TTYs allocated on the host or in
other jails. Effectively, the information printed by \"pstat -t\" may be
leaked.


## Affected packages
 - **FreeBSD-kernel**
    - **Introduced:** 14.0
    - **Fixed:** 14.0_5
    - **Introduced:** 13.2
    - **Fixed:** 13.2_10
