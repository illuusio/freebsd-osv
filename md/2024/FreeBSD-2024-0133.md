# QtNetworkAuth -- predictable seeding of PRNG in QAbstractOAuth
Andy Shaw reports:

> The OAuth1 implementation in QtNetworkAuth created nonces using a PRNG
> that was seeded with a predictable seed.
>
> This means that an attacker that can somehow control the time of the
> first OAuth1 flow of the process has a high chance of predicting the
> nonce used in said OAuth flow.


## Affected packages
 - **qt5-networkauth**
    - **Fixed:** 5.15.13_1
 - **qt6-networkauth**
    - **Fixed:** 6.7.1
