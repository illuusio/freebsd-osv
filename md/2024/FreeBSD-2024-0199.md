# md4c -- DoS attack
cve@mitre.org reports:

> md_analyze_line in md4c.c in md4c 0.4.7 allows attackers to trigger
> use of uninitialized memory, and cause a denial of service via a
> malformed Markdown document.


## Affected packages
 - **md4c**
    - **Fixed:** 0.4.7
