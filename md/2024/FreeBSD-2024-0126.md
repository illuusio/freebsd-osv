# OpenSSL -- Denial of Service vulnerability
The OpenSSL project reports:

> Excessive time spent checking DSA keys and parameters (Low)
>
> Checking excessively long DSA keys or parameters may be very slow.

    - **Fixed:** 3.1.5_4

    - **Fixed:** 3.0.13_4
 - **openssl31-quictls**
