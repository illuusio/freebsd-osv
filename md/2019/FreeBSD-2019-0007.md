# irssi -- Use after free
Irssi reports:

> Use after free when hidden lines were expired from the scroll buffer.
> It may affect the stability of Irssi. (CWE-417, CWE-825)


## Affected packages
 - **irssi**
    - **Introduced:** 1.1.0,1
    - **Fixed:** 1.1.2,1
