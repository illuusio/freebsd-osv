# www/varnish6 -- Denial of Service
The Varnish Team reports:

> A failure in HTTP/1 parsing can allow a remote attacker to trigger an
> assertion in varnish, restarting the daemon and clearing the cache.


## Affected packages
 - **varnish6**
    - **Fixed:** 6.2.1
