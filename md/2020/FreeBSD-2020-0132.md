# nghttp2 -- DoS vulnerability
nghttp2 security advisories:

> The overly large HTTP/2 SETTINGS frame payload causes denial of
> service.
>
> The proof of concept attack involves a malicious client constructing a
> SETTINGS frame with a length of 14,400 bytes (2400 individual settings
> entries) over and over again. The attack causes the CPU to spike at
> 100%.


## Affected packages
 - **nghttp2**
    - **Fixed:** 1.41.0
 - **libnghttp2**
    - **Fixed:** 1.41.0
