# samba -- Multiple Vulnerabilities
The Samba Team reports:

> Four vulnerabilities were fixed in samba:
>
> - CVE-2020-10730: NULL pointer de-reference and use-after-free in
>   Samba AD DC LDAP Server with ASQ, VLV and paged_results
> - CVE-2020-10745: Parsing and packing of NBT and DNS packets can
>   consume excessive CPU in the AD DC (only)
> - CVE-2020-10760: LDAP Use-after-free in Samba AD DC Global Catalog
>   with paged_results and VLV
> - CVE-2020-14303: Empty UDP packet DoS in Samba AD DC nbtd


## Affected packages
 - **samba410**
    - **Fixed:** 4.10.17
 - **samba411**
    - **Fixed:** 4.11.11
 - **samba412**
    - **Fixed:** 4.12.4
