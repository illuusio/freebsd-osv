# Mbed TLS -- Side channel attack on ECDSA
Janos Follath reports:

> Our bignum implementation is not constant time/constant trace, so side
> channel attacks can retrieve the blinded value, factor it (as it is
> smaller than RSA keys and not guaranteed to have only large prime
> factors), and then, by brute force, recover the key.


## Affected packages
 - **mbedtls**
    - **Fixed:** 2.16.4
