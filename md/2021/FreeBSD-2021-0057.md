# asterisk -- Crash when negotiating T.38 with a zero port
The Asterisk project reports:

> When Asterisk sends a re-invite initiating T.38 faxing and the
> endpoint responds with a m=image line and zero port, a crash will
> occur in Asterisk. This is a reoccurrence of AST-2019-004.


## Affected packages
 - **asterisk16**
    - **Fixed:** 16.16.2
 - **asterisk18**
    - **Fixed:** 18.2.2
