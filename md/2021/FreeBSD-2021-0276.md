# rubygem-date -- Regular Expression Denial of Service Vunlerability of Date Parsing Methods
Stanislav Valkanov reports:

> Date\'s parsing methods including `Date.parse` are using Regexps
> internally, some of which are vulnerable against regular expression
> denial of service. Applications and libraries that apply such methods
> to untrusted input may be affected.
