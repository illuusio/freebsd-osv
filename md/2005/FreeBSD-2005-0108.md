# phpmyadmin -- increased privilege vulnerability
The phpMyAdmin team reports:

> Escaping of the \"\_\" character was not properly done, giving a
> wildcard privilege when editing db-specific privileges with
> phpMyAdmin.


## Affected packages
 - **phpmyadmin**
    - **Fixed:** 2.6.1.3
 - **phpMyAdmin**
    - **Fixed:** 2.6.1.3
