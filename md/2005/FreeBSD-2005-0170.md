# racoon -- remote denial-of-service
Sebastian Krahmer discovered that the racoon ISAKMP daemon could be
crashed with a maliciously crafted UDP packet. No authentication is
required in order to perform the attack.


## Affected packages
 - **racoon**
    - **Fixed:** 20050510a
