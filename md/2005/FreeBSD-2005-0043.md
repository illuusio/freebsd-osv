# imlib -- xpm heap buffer overflows and integer overflows
Pavel Kankovsky reports:

> Imlib affected by a variant of CAN-2004-0782 too.
>
> I\'ve discovered more vulnerabilities in Imlib (1.9.13). In
> particular, it appears to be affected by a variant of Chris Evans\'
> libXpm flaw #1 (CAN-2004-0782, see
> http://scary.beasts.org/security/CESA-2004-003.txt). Look at the
> attached image, it kills ee on my 7.3.

The flaws also affect imlib2.


## Affected packages
 - **imlib**
    - **Fixed:** 1.9.15_2
 - **imlib2**
    - **Fixed:** 1.1.2_1
