# mozilla -- insecure permissions for some downloaded files
In a Mozilla bug report, Daniel Kleinsinger writes:

> I was comparing treatment of attachments opened directly from emails
> on different platforms. I discovered that Linux builds save
> attachments in /tmp with world readable rights. This doesn\'t seem
> like a good thing. Couldn\'t someone else logged onto the same machine
> read your attachments?

This could expose the contents of downloaded files or email attachments
to other users on a multi-user system.
