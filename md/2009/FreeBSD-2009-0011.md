# imap-uw -- local buffer overflow vulnerabilities
SANS reports:

> University of Washington \"tmail\" and \"dmail\" are mail deliver
> agents. \"tmail\" and \"dmail\" are exposed to local buffer overflow
> issues because they fail to perform adequate boundary checks on
> user-supplied data.


## Affected packages
 - **imap-uw**
    - **Fixed:** 2007d
