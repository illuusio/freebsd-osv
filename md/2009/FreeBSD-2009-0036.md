# squid -- remote denial of service vulnerability
Squid security advisory 2009:1 reports:

> Due to an internal error Squid is vulnerable to a denial of service
> attack when processing specially crafted requests.
>
> This problem allows any client to perform a denial of service attack
> on the Squid service.


## Affected packages
 - **squid**
    - **Introduced:** 2.7.1
    - **Fixed:** 2.7.6
    - **Introduced:** 3.0.1
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.13
