# BIND -- Remote DoS with certain RPZ configurations
ISC reports:

> Two defects were discovered in ISC\'s BIND 9.8 code. These defects
> only affect BIND 9.8 servers which have recursion enabled and which
> use a specific feature of the software known as Response Policy Zones
> (RPZ) and where the RPZ zone contains a specific rule/action pattern.


## Affected packages
 - **bind98**
    - **Fixed:** 9.8.0.4
