# rubygem-rails -- multiple vulnerabilities
SecurityFocus reports:

> Ruby on Rails is prone to multiple vulnerabilities including
> SQL-injection, information-disclosure, HTTP-header-injection,
> security-bypass and cross-site scripting issues.


## Affected packages
 - **rubygem-rails**
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.10
