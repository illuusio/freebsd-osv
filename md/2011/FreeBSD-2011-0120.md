# XSS issue in MantisBT
> Net.Edit0r from BlACK Hat Group reported an XSS issue in search.php.
> All MantisBT users (including anonymous users that are not logged in
> to public bug trackers) could be impacted by this vulnerability.


## Affected packages
 - **mantis**
    - **Introduced:** 1.2.0
    - **Fixed:** 1.2.7
