# squid -- Denial of Service vulnerability in HTCP
Squid security advisory 2010:2 reports:

> Due to incorrect processing Squid is vulnerable to a denial of service
> attack when receiving specially crafted HTCP packets.
>
> This problem allows any machine to perform a denial of service attack
> on the Squid service when its HTCP port is open.


## Affected packages
 - **squid**
    - **Introduced:** 2.7.1
    - **Fixed:** 2.7.7_4
    - **Introduced:** 3.0.1
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.24
