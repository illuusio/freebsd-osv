# otrs -- information disclosure
The OTRS Project reports:

> An attacker with a valid agent login could manipulate URLs in the
> ticket watch mechanism to see contents of tickets they are not
> permitted to see.


## Affected packages
 - **otrs**
    - **Fixed:** 3.2.8
