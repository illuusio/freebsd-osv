# dropbear -- exposure of sensitive information, DoS
The Dropbear project reports:

> A weakness and a vulnerability have been reported in Dropbear SSH
> Server, which can be exploited by malicious people to disclose certain
> sensitive information and cause a DoS.


## Affected packages
 - **dropbear**
    - **Introduced:** 2012.55
    - **Fixed:** 2013.59
