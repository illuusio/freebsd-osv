# FreeBSD -- OpenSSL multiple vulnerabilities
> A flaw in the OpenSSL handling of OCSP response verification could be
> exploited to cause a denial of service attack.
>
> OpenSSL has a weakness in the handling of CBC ciphersuites in SSL, TLS
> and DTLS. The weakness could reveal plaintext in a timing attack.


## Affected packages
 - **FreeBSD**
    - **Introduced:** 8.3
    - **Fixed:** 8.3_7
    - **Introduced:** 9.0
    - **Fixed:** 9.0_7
    - **Introduced:** 9.1
    - **Fixed:** 9.1_2
