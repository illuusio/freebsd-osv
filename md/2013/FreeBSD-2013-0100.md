# net/openafs -- buffer overflow
Nickolai Zeldovich reports:

> An attacker with the ability to manipulate AFS directory ACLs may
> crash the fileserver hosting that volume. In addition, once a corrupt
> ACL is placed on a fileserver, its existence may crash client
> utilities manipulating ACLs on that server.


## Affected packages
 - **openafs**
    - **Fixed:** 1.6.2.*
