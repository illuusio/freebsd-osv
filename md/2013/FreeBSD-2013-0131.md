# openafs -- single-DES cell-wide key brute force vulnerability
OpenAFS Project reports:

> The small size of the DES key space permits an attacker to brute force
> a cell\'s service key and then forge traffic from any user within the
> cell. The key space search can be performed in under 1 day at a cost
> of around \$100 using publicly available services.


## Affected packages
 - **openafs**
    - **Fixed:** 1.6.5
