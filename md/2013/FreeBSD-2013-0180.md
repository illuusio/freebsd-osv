# OpenTTD -- Denial of service using forcefully crashed aircrafts
The OpenTTD Team reports:

> The problem is caused by incorrectly handling the fact that the
> aircraft circling the corner airport will be outside of the bounds of
> the map. In the \'out of fuel\' crash code the height of the tile
> under the aircraft is determined. In this case that means a tile
> outside of the allocated map array, which could occasionally trigger
> invalid reads.


## Affected packages
 - **openttd**
    - **Introduced:** 0.3.6
    - **Fixed:** 1.3.3
