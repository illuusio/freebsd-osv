# mod_pagespeed -- critical cross-site scripting (XSS) vulnerability
mod_pagespeed developers report:

> Various versions of mod_pagespeed are subject to critical cross-site
> scripting (XSS) vulnerability, CVE-2013-6111. This permits a hostile
> third party to execute JavaScript in users\' browsers in context of
> the domain running mod_pagespeed, which could permit theft of users\'
> cookies or data on the site.


## Affected packages
 - **mod_pagespeed**
    - **Fixed:** 1.2.24.2,1
