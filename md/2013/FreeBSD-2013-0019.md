# OpenSSL -- TLS 1.1, 1.2 denial of service
OpenSSL security team reports:

> A flaw in the OpenSSL handling of CBC mode ciphersuites in TLS 1.1 and
> TLS 1.2 on AES-NI supporting platforms can be exploited in a DoS
> attack.
>
> A flaw in the OpenSSL handling of OCSP response verification can be
> exploited in a denial of service attack.


## Affected packages
 - **openssl**
    - **Fixed:** 1.0.1_6
