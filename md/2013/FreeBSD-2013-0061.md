# OpenVPN -- potential side-channel/timing attack when comparing HMACs
The OpenVPN project reports:

> OpenVPN 2.3.0 and earlier running in UDP mode are subject to chosen
> ciphertext injection due to a non-constant-time HMAC comparison
> function.


## Affected packages
 - **openvpn**
    - **Fixed:** 2.0.9_4
    - **Introduced:** 2.1.0
    - **Fixed:** 2.2.2_2
    - **Introduced:** 2.3.0
    - **Fixed:** 2.3.1
