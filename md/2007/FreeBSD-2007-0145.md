# Squid -- Denial of Service Vulnerability
Squid secuirty advisory reports:

> Due to incorrect bounds checking Squid is vulnerable to a denial of
> service check during some cache update reply processing.
>
> This problem allows any client trusted to use the service to perform a
> denial of service attack on the Squid service.


## Affected packages
 - **squid**
    - **Introduced:** 2.0
    - **Fixed:** 2.6.16_1
    - **Introduced:** 3.*
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.r1.20071001_1
