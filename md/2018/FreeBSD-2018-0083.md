# slurm-wlm -- SQL Injection attacks against SlurmDBD
SchedMD reports:

> Several issues were discovered with incomplete sanitization of
> user-provided text strings, which could potentially lead to SQL
> injection attacks against SlurmDBD itself. Such exploits could lead to
> a loss of accounting data, or escalation of user privileges on the
> cluster.


## Affected packages
 - **slurm-wlm**
    - **Fixed:** 17.02.10
