# Gitlab -- multiple vulnerabilities
GitLab reports:

> Confidential issue comments in Slack, Mattermost, and webhook
> integrations.
>
> Persistent XSS in milestones data-milestone-id.
>
> Persistent XSS in filename of merge request.


## Affected packages
 - **gitlab**
    - **Introduced:** 10.6.0
    - **Fixed:** 10.6.3
    - **Introduced:** 10.5.0
    - **Fixed:** 10.5.7
    - **Introduced:** 8.6
    - **Fixed:** 10.4.7
