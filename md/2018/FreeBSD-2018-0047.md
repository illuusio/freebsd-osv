# bro -- out of bounds write allows remote DOS
Frank Meier:

> Bro before Bro v2.5.2 is vulnerable to an out of bounds write in the
> ContentLine analyzer allowing remote attackers to cause a denial of
> service (crash) and possibly other exploitation.


## Affected packages
 - **bro**
    - **Fixed:** 2.5.2
