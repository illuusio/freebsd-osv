# libtorrent -- remote DoS
X-cela reports:

> Calls into build_benocde that use %zu could crash on 64 bit machines
> due to the size change of size_t. Someone can force READ_ENC_IA to
> fail allowing an internal_error to be thrown and bring down the
> client.


## Affected packages
 - **libtorrent**
    - **Fixed:** 0.13.6_5
