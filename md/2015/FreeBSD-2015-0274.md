# powerdns -- denial of service
PowerDNS reports:

> A bug was found in our DNS packet parsing/generation code, which, when
> exploited, can cause individual threads (disabling service) or whole
> processes (allowing a supervisor to restart them) to crash with just
> one or a few query packets.


## Affected packages
 - **powerdns**
    - **Introduced:** 3.4.0
    - **Fixed:** 3.4.6
