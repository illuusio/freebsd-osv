# qemu -- Heap overflow in QEMU PCNET controller, allowing guest to host escape (CVE-2015-3209)
The QEMU security team reports:

> A guest which has access to an emulated PCNET network device (e.g.
> with \"model=pcnet\" in their VIF configuration) can exploit this
> vulnerability to take over the qemu process elevating its privilege to
> that of the qemu process.


## Affected packages
 - **qemu**
    - **Fixed:** 0.11.1_20
    - **Introduced:** 0.12
