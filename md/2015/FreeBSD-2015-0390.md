# libressl -- NULL pointer dereference
The OpenBSD project reports:

> A NULL pointer deference could be triggered by a crafted certificate
> sent to services configured to verify client certificates on TLS/SSL
> connections.


## Affected packages
 - **libressl**
    - **Fixed:** 2.2.5
    - **Introduced:** 2.3.0
    - **Fixed:** 2.3.1_1
