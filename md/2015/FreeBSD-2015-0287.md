# openldap -- denial of service vulnerability
Denis Andzakovic reports:

> By sending a crafted packet, an attacker may cause the OpenLDAP server
> to reach an assert(9 9 statement, crashing the daemon.


## Affected packages
 - **openldap-server**
    - **Fixed:** 2.4.42_1
