# png -- heap overflow for 32-bit builds
32-bit builds of PNG library are vulnerable to an unsigned integer
overflow that is triggered by a crafted wide interlaced images. Overflow
results in a heap corruption that will crash the application and may
lead to the controlled overwrite of a selected portions of process
address space.


## Affected packages
 - **png**
    - **Introduced:** 1.2.6
    - **Fixed:** 1.5.21
    - **Introduced:** 1.6
    - **Fixed:** 1.6.16
