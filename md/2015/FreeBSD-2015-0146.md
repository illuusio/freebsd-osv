# rubygem-bson -- DoS and possible injection
Phill MV reports:

> By submitting a specially crafted string to a service relying on the
> bson rubygem, an attacker may trigger denials of service or even
> inject data into victim\'s MongoDB instances.


## Affected packages
 - **rubygem-bson**
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.4
