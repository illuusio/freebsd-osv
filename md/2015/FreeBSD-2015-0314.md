# gdk-pixbuf2 -- head overflow and DoS
reports:

> We found a heap overflow and a DoS in the gdk-pixbuf implementation
> triggered by the scaling of tga file.

> We found a heap overflow in the gdk-pixbuf implementation triggered by
> the scaling of gif file.


## Affected packages
 - **gdk-pixbuf2**
    - **Fixed:** 2.32.1
