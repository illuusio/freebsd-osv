# gdk-pixbuf2 -- heap overflow and DoS affecting Firefox and other programs
gustavo.grieco@imag.fr reports:

> We found a heap overflow and a DoS in the gdk-pixbuf implementation
> triggered by the scaling of a malformed bmp.


## Affected packages
 - **gdk-pixbuf2**
    - **Fixed:** 2.31.2_2
