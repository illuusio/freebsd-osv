# openjpeg -- use-after-free vulnerability
Feist Josselin reports:

> Use-after-free was found in openjpeg. The vuln is fixed in version
> 2.1.1 and was located in opj_j2k_write_mco function.


## Affected packages
 - **openjpeg**
    - **Fixed:** 2.1.1
