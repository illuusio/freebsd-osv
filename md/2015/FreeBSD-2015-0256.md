# unreal -- denial of service
Unreal reports:

> Summary: If SASL support is enabled in UnrealIRCd (this is not the
> default) and is also enabled in your services package then a malicious
> user with a services account can cause UnrealIRCd to crash.


## Affected packages
 - **Unreal**
    - **Introduced:** 3.2.10
    - **Fixed:** 3.2.10.5
