# ntp -- multiple vulnerabilities
ntp.org reports:

> - \[Sec 2779\] ntpd accepts unauthenticated packets with symmetric key
>   crypto.
> - \[Sec 2781\] Authentication doesn\'t protect symmetric associations
>   against DoS attacks.


## Affected packages
 - **ntp**
    - **Fixed:** 4.2.8p2
 - **ntp-devel**
    - **Fixed:** 4.3.14
 - **FreeBSD**
    - **Introduced:** 10.1
    - **Fixed:** 10.1_9
    - **Introduced:** 9.3
    - **Fixed:** 9.3_13
    - **Introduced:** 8.4
    - **Fixed:** 8.4_27
