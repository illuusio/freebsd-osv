# xen-kernel -- CPU lockup during exception delivery
The Xen Project reports:

> A malicious HVM guest administrator can cause a denial of service.
> Specifically, prevent use of a physical CPU for a significant, perhaps
> indefinite period. If a host watchdog (Xen or dom0) is in use, this
> can lead to a watchdog timeout and consequently a reboot of the host.
> If another, innocent, guest, is configured with a watchdog, this issue
> can lead to a reboot of such a guest.


## Affected packages
 - **xen-kernel**
    - **Fixed:** 4.5.2
