# h2o -- HTTP/2 Rapid Reset attack vulnerability
Kazuo Okuhu reports:

> H2O is vulnerable to the HTTP/2 Rapid Reset attack. An attacker might
> be able to consume more than adequate amount of processing power of
> h2o and the backend servers by mounting the attack.


## Affected packages
 - **h2o**
    - **Fixed:** 2.2.6
 - **h2o-devel**
    - **Fixed:** 2.3.0.d.20231010
