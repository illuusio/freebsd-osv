# OpenSSL -- potential loss of confidentiality
The OpenSSL team reports:

> Moderate severity: A bug has been identified in the processing of key
> and initialisation vector (IV) lengths. This can lead to potential
> truncation or overruns during the initialisation of some symmetric
> ciphers.


## Affected packages
 - **openssl**
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.12,1
 - **openssl31**
    - **Fixed:** 3.1.4
 - **openssl-quictls**
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.12
