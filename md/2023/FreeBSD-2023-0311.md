# x11/libXpm multiple vulnerabilities
The X.Org project reports:

> 
>
> CVE-2023-43788: Out of bounds read in XpmCreateXpmImageFromBuffer
> :   An out-of-bounds read is located in ParseComment() when reading
>     from a memory buffer instead of a file, as it continued to look
>     for the closing comment marker past the end of the buffer.
>
> CVE-2023-43789: Out of bounds read on XPM with corrupted colormap
> :   A corrupted colormap section may cause libXpm to read out of
>     bounds.


## Affected packages
 - **libXpm**
    - **Fixed:** 3.5.17
