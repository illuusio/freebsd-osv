# py-tensorflow -- denial of service vulnerability
Kang Hong Jin, Neophytos Christou, 刘力源 and Pattarakrit Rattankul
report:

> Another instance of CVE-2022-35935, where \`SobolSample\` is
> vulnerable to a denial of service via assumed scalar inputs, was found
> and fixed.

Pattarakrit Rattankul reports:

> Another instance of CVE-2022-35991, where \`TensorListScatter\` and
> \`TensorListScatterV2\` crash via non scalar inputs
> in\`element_shape\`, was found in eager mode and fixed.
