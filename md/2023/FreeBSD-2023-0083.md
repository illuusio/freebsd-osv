# dino -- Insufficient message sender validation in Dino
Dino team reports:

> Dino before 0.2.3, 0.3.x before 0.3.2, and 0.4.x before 0.4.2 allows
> attackers to modify the personal bookmark store via a crafted message.
> The attacker can change the display of group chats or force a victim
> to join a group chat; the victim may then be tricked into disclosing
> sensitive information.


## Affected packages
 - **dino**
    - **Fixed:** 0.4.2
