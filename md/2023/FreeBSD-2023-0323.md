# squid -- Multiple vulnerabilities
The squid-cache project reports:

> - Denial of Service in FTP
> - Request/Response smuggling in HTTP/1.1 and ICAP
> - Denial of Service in HTTP Digest Authentication


## Affected packages
 - **squid**
    - **Fixed:** 6.4
