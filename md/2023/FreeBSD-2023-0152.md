# h2o -- Malformed HTTP/1.1 causes Out-of-Memory Denial of Service
Elijah Glover reports:

> Malformed HTTP/1.1 requests can crash worker processes. occasionally
> locking up child workers and causing denial of service, and an outage
> dropping any open connections.


## Affected packages
 - **h2o**
    - **Fixed:** 2.2.6
 - **h2o-devel**
    - **Fixed:** 2.3.0.d.20230427
