# py39-sentry-sdk -- sensitive cookies leak
Tom Wolters reports:

> When using the Django integration of the Sentry SDK in a specific
> configuration it is possible to leak sensitive cookies values,
> including the session cookie to Sentry.
>
> These sensitive cookies could then be used by someone with access to
> your Sentry issues to impersonate or escalate their privileges within
> your application.


## Affected packages
 - **py39-sentry-sdk**
    - **Fixed:** 1.14.0
