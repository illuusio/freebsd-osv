# libsndfile_project -- Integer overflow in dataend calculation
cve@mitre.org reports:

> Multiple signed integers overflow in function au_read_header in
> src/au.c and in functions mat4_open and mat4_read_header in src/mat4.c
> in Libsndfile, allows an attacker to cause Denial of Service or other
> unspecified impacts.


## Affected packages
 - **libsndfile**
    - **Fixed:** 1.2.2_1
