# xen-kernel -- x86: Privilege escalation in PV guests
The Xen Project reports:

> The PV pagetable code has fast-paths for making updates to
> pre-existing pagetable entries, to skip expensive re-validation in
> safe cases (e.g. clearing only Access/Dirty bits). The bits considered
> safe were too broad, and not actually safe.
>
> A malicious PV guest administrator can escalate their privilege to
> that of the host.


## Affected packages
 - **xen-kernel**
    - **Fixed:** 4.7.0_3
