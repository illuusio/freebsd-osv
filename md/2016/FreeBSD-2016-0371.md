# wolfssl -- leakage of private key information
Florian Weimer of Redhat discovered that an optimization in RSA
signature validation can result in disclosure of the server\'s private
key under certain fault conditions.


## Affected packages
 - **wolfssl**
    - **Fixed:** 3.6.8
