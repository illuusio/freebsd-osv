# krb5 -- KDC denial of service vulnerability
Major changes in krb5 1.14.3 and krb5 1.13.6:

> Fix a rare KDC denial of service vulnerability when anonymous client
> principals are restricted to obtaining TGTs only \[CVE-2016-3120\] .


## Affected packages
 - **krb5-113**
    - **Fixed:** 1.13.6
 - **krb5-114**
    - **Fixed:** 1.14.3
