# samba -- client side SMB2/3 required signing can be downgraded
Samba team reports:

> A man in the middle attack can disable client signing over SMB2/3,
> even if enforced by configuration parameters.


## Affected packages
 - **samba4**
    - **Introduced:** 4.0.0
    - **Fixed:** 4.0.26
 - **samba41**
    - **Introduced:** 4.1.0
    - **Fixed:** 4.1.23
 - **samba42**

    - **Fixed:** 4.2.14

    - **Introduced:** 4.3.0
    - **Fixed:** 4.3.11
 - **samba44**
    - **Introduced:** 4.4.0
    - **Fixed:** 4.4.5
