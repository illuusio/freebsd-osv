# py-rsa -- Bleichenbacher'06 signature forgery vulnerability
Filippo Valsorda reports:

> python-rsa is vulnerable to a straightforward variant of the
> Bleichenbacher\'06 attack against RSA signature verification with low
> public exponent.


## Affected packages
 - **py27-rsa**
    - **Fixed:** 3.3
 - **py32-rsa**
    - **Fixed:** 3.3
 - **py33-rsa**
    - **Fixed:** 3.3
 - **py34-rsa**
    - **Fixed:** 3.3
 - **py35-rsa**
    - **Fixed:** 3.3
