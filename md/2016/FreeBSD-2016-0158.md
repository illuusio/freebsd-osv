# pcre -- stack buffer overflow
Philip Hazel reports:

> PCRE does not validate that handling the (\*ACCEPT) verb will occur
> within the bounds of the cworkspace stack buffer, leading to a stack
> buffer overflow.


## Affected packages
 - **pcre**
    - **Fixed:** 8.38
 - **pcre2**
    - **Fixed:** 10.20_1
