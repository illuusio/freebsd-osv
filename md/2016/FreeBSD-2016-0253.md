# dnsmasq -- denial of service
reports:

> Dnsmasq before 2.76 allows remote servers to cause a denial of service
> (crash) via a reply with an empty DNS address that has an (1) A or (2)
> AAAA record defined locally.


## Affected packages
 - **dnsmasq**
    - **Fixed:** 2.76,1
 - **dnsmasq-devel**
    - **Fixed:** 2.76.0test1
