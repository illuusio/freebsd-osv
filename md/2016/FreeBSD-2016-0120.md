# xen-kernel -- VMX: intercept issue with INVLPG on non-canonical address
The Xen Project reports:

> While INVLPG does not cause a General Protection Fault when used on a
> non-canonical address, INVVPID in its \"individual address\" variant,
> which is used to back the intercepted INVLPG in certain cases, fails
> in such cases. Failure of INVVPID results in a hypervisor bug check.
>
> A malicious guest can crash the host, leading to a Denial of Service.


## Affected packages
 - **xen-kernel**
    - **Introduced:** 3.3
    - **Fixed:** 4.5.2_2
