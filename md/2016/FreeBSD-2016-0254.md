# expat2 -- denial of service
Adam Maris reports:

> It was found that original patch for issues CVE-2015-1283 and
> CVE-2015-2716 used overflow checks that could be optimized out by some
> compilers applying certain optimization settings, which can cause the
> vulnerability to remain even after applying the patch.


## Affected packages
 - **expat**
    - **Fixed:** 2.1.1_2
