# botan -- multiple vulnerabilities
Jack Lloyd reports:

> Botan 1.10.13 has been released backporting some side channel
> protections for ECDSA signatures (CVE-2016-2849) and PKCS #1 RSA
> decryption (CVE-2015-7827).
