# openafs -- local DoS vulnerability
The OpenAFS development team reports:

> Avoid a potential denial of service issue, by fixing a bug in pioctl
> logic that allowed a local user to overrun a kernel buffer with a
> single NUL byte.


## Affected packages
 - **openafs**
    - **Fixed:** 1.6.16
