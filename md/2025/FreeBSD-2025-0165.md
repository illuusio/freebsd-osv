# libxml2 -- Use After Free
cve@mitre.org reports:

> libxml2 before 2.12.10 and 2.13.x before 2.13.6 has a use-after-free
> in xmlSchemaIDCFillNodeTables and xmlSchemaBubbleIDCNodeTables in
> xmlschemas.c. To exploit this, a crafted XML document must be
> validated against an XML schema with certain identity constraints, or
> a crafted XML schema must be used.


## Affected packages
 - **libxml2**
    - **Fixed:** 2.11.9_1
    - **Introduced:** 2.12.0
    - **Fixed:** 2.12.10
    - **Introduced:** 2.13.0
    - **Fixed:** 2.13.6
