# step-certificates -- Authorization Bypass in ACME and SCEP Provisioners
smallstep reports:

> An attacker can bypass authorization checks and force a Step CA ACME
> or SCEP provisioner to create certificates without completing certain
> protocol authorization checks.


## Affected packages
 - **step-certificates**
    - **Fixed:** 0.29.0
