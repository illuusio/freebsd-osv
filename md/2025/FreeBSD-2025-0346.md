# RT -- CSV injection
Gareth Watkin-Jones from 4armed reports:

> RT is vulnerable to CSV injection via ticket values with special
> characters that are exported to a TSV from search results. Thanks to
> Gareth Watkin-Jones from 4armed for reporting this finding.
