# Mozilla -- null pointer dereference
security@mozilla.org reports:

> A null pointer dereference may have inadvertently occurred in
> \`pk12util\`, and specifically in the \`SEC_ASN1DecodeItem_Util\`
> function, when handling malformed or improperly formatted input files.


## Affected packages
 - **firefox**
    - **Fixed:** 133.0,2
 - **thunderbird**
    - **Fixed:** 133.0
