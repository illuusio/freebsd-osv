# minio -- Privilege Escalation via Session Policy Bypass in Service Accounts and STS
mino reports:

> A privilege escalation vulnerability allows service accounts and STS
> (Security Token Service) accounts with restricted session policies to
> bypass their inline policy restrictions when performing \"own\"
> account operations, specifically when creating new service accounts
> for the same user.


## Affected packages
 - **minio**
