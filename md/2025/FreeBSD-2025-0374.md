# MongoDB -- Improper Check for Unusual or Exceptional Conditions
https://jira.mongodb.org/browse/SERVER-101230 reports:

> The KMIP response parser built into mongo binaries is overly tolerant
> of certain malformed packets, and may parse them into invalid objects.
> Later reads of this object can result in read access violations.


## Affected packages
 - **mongodb70**
    - **Fixed:** 7.0.22
