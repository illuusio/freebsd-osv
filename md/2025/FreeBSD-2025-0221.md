# MongoDB -- may be susceptible to privilege escalation due to $mergeCursors stage
cna@mongodb.com reports:

> An unauthorized user may leverage a specially crafted aggregation
> pipeline to access data without proper authorization due to improper
> handling of the \$mergeCursors stage in MongoDB Server. This may lead
> to access to data without further authorisation.


## Affected packages
 - **mongodb60**
    - **Fixed:** 6.0.22
 - **mongodb70**
    - **Fixed:** 7.0.20
