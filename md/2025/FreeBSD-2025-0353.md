# py-social-auth-app-django -- Unsafe account association
Michal Čihař reports:

> Upon authentication, the user could be associated by e-mail even if
> the associate_by_email pipeline was not included. This could lead to
> account compromise when a third-party authentication service does not
> validate provided e-mail addresses or doesn\'t require unique e-mail
> addresses.
