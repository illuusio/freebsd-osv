# MongoDB -- Missing Authorization
https://jira.mongodb.org/browse/SERVER-103582 reports:

> A user with access to the cluster with a limited set of privilege
> actions may be able to terminate queries that are being executed by
> other users. This may cause a denial of service by preventing a
> fraction of queries from successfully completing.


## Affected packages
 - **mongodb80**
    - **Fixed:** 8.0.14
 - **mongodb70**
    - **Fixed:** 7.0.26
