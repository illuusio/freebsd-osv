# Mozilla -- mitigation bypass vulnerability
security@mozilla.org reports:

> The vulnerability has been rated as having moderate impact, affecting
> both confidentiality and integrity with low severity, while having no
> impact on availability. For Thunderbird specifically, the
> vulnerability cannot be exploited through email as scripting is
> disabled when reading mail, but remains a potential risk in browser or
> browser-like contexts


## Affected packages
 - **firefox**
    - **Fixed:** 143.0,2
 - **thunderbird**
    - **Fixed:** 143.0
