# Django -- multiple vulnerabilities
Django reports:

> CVE-2025-59681: Potential SQL injection in QuerySet.annotate(),
> alias(), aggregate(), and extra() on MySQL and MariaDB.

> CVE-2025-59682: Potential partial directory-traversal via
> archive.extract().
