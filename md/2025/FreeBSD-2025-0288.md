# Gitlab -- Vulnerabilities
Gitlab reports:

> Denial of Service issue in SAML Responses impacts GitLab CE/EE
>
> Server-Side Request Forgery issue in Webhook custom header impacts
> GitLab CE/EE
>
> Denial of Service issue in User-Controllable Fields impacts GitLab
> CE/EE
>
> Denial of Service issue in endpoint file upload impacts GitLab CE/EE
>
> Denial of Service issue in token listing operations impacts GitLab
> CE/EE
>
> Information disclosure issue in runner endpoints impacts GitLab CE/EE

 - **gitlab-ce**
