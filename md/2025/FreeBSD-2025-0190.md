# Grafana -- DingDing contact points exposed in Grafana Alerting
Grafana Labs reports:

> An incident occurred where the DingDing alerting integration URL was
> inadvertently exposed to viewers due to a setting oversight, which we
> learned about through a [bug bounty
> report](https://grafana.com/blog/2023/05/04/introducing-the-grafana-labs-bug-bounty-program/).
>
> The CVSS 3.0 score for this vulnerability is 4.3 (Medium).
