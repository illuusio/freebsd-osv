# squid -- denial of service
Squid developers report:

> Due to missing input validation Squid cachemgr.cgi tool is vulnerable
> to a denial of service attack when processing specially crafted
> requests.
>
> This problem allows any client able to reach the cachemgr.cgi to
> perform a denial of service attack on the service host.
>
> The nature of the attack may cause secondary effects through resource
> consumption on the host server.
