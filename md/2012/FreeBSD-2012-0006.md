# PowerDNS -- Denial of Service Vulnerability
The PowerDNS Team reports:

> Using well crafted UDP packets, one or more PowerDNS servers could be
> made to enter a tight packet loop, causing temporary denial of
> service.


## Affected packages
 - **powerdns**
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.1
 - **powerdns-devel**
    - **Fixed:** 3.0.1
