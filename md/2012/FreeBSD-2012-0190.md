# xlockmore -- local exploit
Ignatios Souvatzis of NetBSD reports:

> Due to an error in the dclock screensaver in xlockmore, users who
> explicitly use this screensaver or a random mix of screensavers using
> something like \"xlockmore -mode random\" may have their screen
> unlocked unexpectedly at a random time.


## Affected packages
 - **xlockmore**
    - **Fixed:** 5.40_1
 - **ja-xlockmore**
    - **Fixed:** 5.40_1
