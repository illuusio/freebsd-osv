# isc-dhcp-server -- DoS in DHCPv6
ISC reports:

> Due to improper handling of a DHCPv6 lease structure, ISC DHCP servers
> that are serving IPv6 address pools AND using Dynamic DNS can
> encounter a segmentation fault error while updating lease status under
> certain conditions.
>
> The potential exists for this condition to be intentionally triggered,
> resulting in effective denial of service to clients expecting service
> from the affected server.


## Affected packages
 - **isc-dhcp42-server**
    - **Fixed:** 4.2.3_2
